Authentic Being

An 8-Week Container for Men Who Want to Embrace Their Power, Build Discipline, and Become the Man They Know They Could Be

Even if you feel stuck in bad habits, lack direction, or keep sabotaging the life you say you want.

The Core Idea

It's tough to deal with the onslaught of messaging, temptation, and pressure of modern male life. We are societally ill-equipped to hold tension.

This tension between:

  • who we are and who we could be
  • desire and discipline
  • freedom and responsibility
  • comfort and growth

Instead, we learn to escape tension.

Through distraction. Through substances. Through endless thinking instead of action.

But masculine maturity is something else entirely.

It is the ability to hold tension without collapsing into impulse.

This container is about developing that capacity.

Program Structure

  • 8 weekly 2-hour group calls
  • A small cohort of thoughtful men
  • Guest speakers throughout the program
  • 4 private 1-on-1 sessions

This container is limited to a small group of men so the work can remain honest, direct, and accountable.

What You'll Gain

  • A framework to understand why you keep drifting away from the life you want - and how to correct it.
  • A deeper understanding of the hidden forces shaping your behavior and decisions.
  • The ability to recognize and break conditioning loops that have been running your habits for years.
  • The discipline to hold tension without collapsing into impulse.
  • A healthier relationship to desire, power, and responsibility.
  • A clearer sense of direction and authorship over your life.

A Personal Note

This container comes from over a decade of personal inquiry.

I began exploring these questions because I was trying to solve them for myself.

I wanted to understand why intelligent, capable men still drift. Why discipline is so difficult. Why desire so often pulls us away from the lives we want to build.

Have I mastered all of this perfectly?

Of course not.

But I have spent an enormous amount of time building a philosophical and psychological framework that has helped me tremendously - and that I believe can help others as well.

This work matters deeply to me.

More than my career in software engineering.

More than music or DJing.

Helping men become more aligned, responsible, and powerful in their lives is the most meaningful work I know how to do.

Curriculum Overview

  • Week 1 - Being and Identity: Understanding what it means to exist as a self and consciously shape your identity as a man.
  • Week 2 - Tension: Exploring the relational nature of life and identifying the tensions that shape your decisions and direction.
  • Week 3 - Conditioning: Understanding how habits form and how stimulus loops quietly shape your behavior.
  • Week 4 - Masculine Containment: Developing impulse control, somatic awareness, and the capacity to hold energy rather than leak it.
  • Week 5 - Shadow Integration: Meeting the hidden and unconscious forces influencing your life with honesty and compassion.
  • Week 6 - Leaking Power: Identifying where distraction, avoidance, and addiction undermine your life.
  • Week 7 - The Morality of Power: Understanding responsibility, trust in oneself, and the ethical use of personal power.
  • Week 8 - Assuming the Seat of Self: Developing a grounded sense of authorship and inner alignment.
