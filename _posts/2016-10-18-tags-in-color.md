I’ve had an idea for a while. To assign colors to tags and style posts accordingly. Perhaps blend the colors when a post has more than one tag. For example, if blue is for software engineering, and yellow is for personal, then a personal post about software engineering would be green. 

I don’t know - it could lead to some cool UIs. 

For example, a person can sign up for an account on this platform where posts are color coded and she can blend colors to pick what she is interested in consuming. Maybe big colored circles you click to indicate interest? 

At any rate, I can flush this idea out eventually. 

Here are the color assignments I have as of yet: 

Software Engineering - Blue

Cooking - Orange

Music - Red

Books - Green

Personal - Yellow

Family & Friends - Purple
