Forge Privacy Policy

Forge does not collect any user data or personal information. This extension does not require any permissions to access user data - only to manage browser tabs and windows. It does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

We do not track your browsing history, search queries, or any other information related to your use of this extension. We respect your privacy and do not engage in any data collection practices that could compromise your privacy or security.

We may collect non-personal information such as the number of installations, version numbers, and geographic location, solely for the purpose of improving the functionality and user experience of the extension. This information is collected anonymously and is not linked to any identifiable user data.

We do not sell, share, or distribute any user data or personal information to any third parties. We do not use any user data for advertising or any other commercial purposes.

We maintain the right to update our policies at any time. We will notify you of any changes to our privacy policy by posting the new policy on this page.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at tablist.team@gmail.com
